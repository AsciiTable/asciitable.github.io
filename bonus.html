Publication: Habibi, R., Maram, S., Pfau, J., Wei, J., Sisodiya, S., Kashani, A., Carstensdottir, E., Seif El-Nasr, M. (2022). Urban Air Mobility CAVE Research

A simulation developed in a CAVE VR environment to observe how air traffic control might work with air taxis in San Francisco, CA and train new pilots to fly the type of aircraft required for urban settings. Publication: P. Marayong, P. Shankar, J. Wei, H. Nguyen, T. Z. Strybel and V. Battiste, "Urban Air Mobility System Testbed using CAVE Virtual Reality Environment," 2020 IEEE Aerospace Conference, 2020, pp. 1-7, doi: 10.1109/AERO47225.2020.9172534.
